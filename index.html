Step 1: Add Your Willmar Account (if not already added)

  1. Open Settings on your Android device
  2. Go to Accounts (or Passwords & accounts)
  3. Tap Add account → Google
  4. Enter your email: username@willmar.k12.mn.us
  5. Sign in with your Willmar password

Step 2: Download School Certificate

📥 Download Willmar RADIUS Certificate

After downloading: Tap the file and tap Install.

Step 3: Install Network Profile (Recommended)

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Search for and install HPE Aruba Networking Onboard
  3. Open the app
  4. Sign in with your willmar.k12.mn.us account
  5. Follow the prompts — it will install the profile and certificate

Step 4: Connect to Secure Network

  1. Forget the "Enroll" network
  2. Select your Staff or Student network
  3. Enter your Willmar username and password when prompted

You're done! Contact tech support if you have any issues.
